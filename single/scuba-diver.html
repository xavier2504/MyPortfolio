Scuba Diver

To boost Tudor boutiques, we have played on the theme of the seabed. Indeed, I worked on the universe of the scuba diver of the 70s, a very rich and important topic in Tudor. The intention was to arouse curiosity as the customer walks through the store. The goal is for him to take a selfi with the diver accompanied by the current watch model. This project is available in different formats: 1/1 scale, for libraries and displays.
