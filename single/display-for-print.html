Display for print

In recent years, Breitling has changed its communication.

It was therefore necessary to adapt the displays to the new visual identity. The intention was to make a recall of the new industrial style shops. To do this, the display consists of a grid with the Breitling «B» highlighted and a yellow bar to limit the space between support and print. This concept makes it possible to preserve the iconic yellow of Breitling and to incorporate visuals which integrate easily and adapte according to the watch models displayed on the front.
